Best AI Apps for Students in 2026

Students are increasingly using AI apps to boost productivity and learn faster. Here’s a list of the best AI apps for students in 2026.

  • ChatGPT: AI chatbot for answering questions and generating study notes. Pros: Easy to use, fast answers. Cons: Needs internet. Best for: Homework and learning assistance.
  • Quizlet AI: AI-powered flashcards for exam prep. Pros: Interactive, gamified. Cons: Limited offline use. Best for: Memorization and revision.
  • Notion AI: AI assistant inside Notion for notes, summaries, and planning. Pros: Versatile, organized. Cons: Requires learning curve. Best for: Project management and notes.

Top Free Video Editing Apps for Android (2026)

Editing videos is easier than ever with free apps. Here’s a list of the top free video editing apps for Android in 2026.

  • CapCut: Easy templates, best for beginners. Pros: Intuitive, lots of effects. Cons: Some features require account. Best for: Social media creators.
  • VN Video Editor: Professional-level editing without watermark. Pros: Smooth timeline editing, no watermark. Cons: Slightly complex interface. Best for: Serious creators.
  • InShot: Quick edits for Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube. Pros: Fast, simple. Cons: Ads in free version. Best for: Quick social media edits.

Best Photo Editing Apps for Low-End Phones

Not everyone has high-end phones, but photo editing is still possible. Here are the best photo editing apps for low-end phones.

  • Snapseed: Lightweight, easy to use. Pros: Professional filters, free. Cons: Some features hidden. Best for: Beginner & intermediate users.
  • Pixlr: Fast, simple, and fun effects. Pros: Minimal lag, free. Cons: Ads in free version. Best for: Quick social media edits.
  • PhotoDirector: Creative edits with AI tools. Pros: AI-powered tools, smooth interface. Cons: Some premium features locked. Best for: Creative editing.

AI Apps That Convert Text to Video

Want to turn your text into videos automatically? Here are the best AI apps that convert text to video in 2026.

  • Lumen5: AI video creation from text. Pros: Templates, easy to use. Cons: Watermark in free version. Best for: Marketing and social content.
  • Pictory AI: Converts blog or text scripts into short videos. Pros: Fast, multiple templates. Cons: Limited free plan. Best for: Bloggers and creators.
  • InVideo: AI-assisted video creation. Pros: Customization, library of assets. Cons: Free version has watermark. Best for: Short social videos.
