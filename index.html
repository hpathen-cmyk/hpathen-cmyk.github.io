Best AI Apps for Students in 2026

Students are increasingly using AI apps to boost productivity and learn faster. Here’s a list of the best AI apps for students in 2026.

  • ChatGPT: AI chatbot for answering questions and generating study notes. Pros: Easy to use, fast answers. Cons: Needs internet.
  • Quizlet AI: AI-powered flashcards for exam prep. Pros: Interactive, gamified. Cons: Limited offline use.
  • Notion AI: AI assistant inside Notion for notes, summaries, and planning. Pros: Versatile, organized. Cons: Learning curve.

Top Free Video Editing Apps for Android (2026)

Editing videos is easier than ever with free apps. Here’s a list of the top free video editing apps for Android in 2026.

  • CapCut: Easy templates, best for beginners. Pros: Intuitive, lots of effects. Cons: Some features require account.
  • VN Video Editor: Professional-level editing without watermark. Pros: Smooth timeline editing, no watermark. Cons: Slightly complex interface.
  • InShot: Quick edits for Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube. Pros: Fast, simple. Cons: Ads in free version.

Best Photo Editing Apps for Low-End Phones

Not everyone has high-end phones, but photo editing is still possible. Here are the best photo editing apps for low-end phones.

  • Snapseed: Lightweight, easy to use. Pros: Professional filters, free. Cons: Some hidden features.
  • Pixlr: Fast, simple, and fun effects. Pros: Minimal lag, free. Cons: Ads in free version.
  • PhotoDirector: Creative edits with AI tools. Pros: Smooth interface. Cons: Some premium features locked.

AI Apps That Convert Text to Video

Want to turn your text into videos automatically? Here are the best AI apps that convert text to video in 2026.

  • Lumen5: AI video creation from text. Pros: Templates, easy to use. Cons: Watermark in free version.
  • Pictory AI: Converts blog or text scripts into short videos. Pros: Fast, multiple templates. Cons: Limited free plan.
  • InVideo: AI-assisted video creation. Pros: Customization, library of assets. Cons: Free version has watermark.
